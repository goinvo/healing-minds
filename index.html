Understanding Psilocybin

As states across the U.S. explore how to legalize and regulate psilocybin, eight distinct policy models have emerged. This matrix outlines these options—ranging from FDA-approved use to state-run monopolies to help identify promising paths toward making psychedelic-assisted mental health treatment more accessible.
